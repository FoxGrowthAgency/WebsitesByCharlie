Why I'm Doing This

I'm currently studying Software Engineering at university, and while I love learning the theory, I know the best way to get better is by building real things for real people.

I build clean, one-page websites that work perfectly on phones and computers. I work with small businesses, tradies, and local shops — people who need a simple online presence without the big agency price tag.

I'm not a big agency. I don't have a team of twenty or fancy offices. I'm just someone who wants to help your business look professional online while I improve my craft.

This helps me grow as a developer while giving local businesses an affordable way to get online.
